Professional network and relationship support

Networking and relationship support describes the active role Cheetah innovation takes to develop connections to resources that Cheetah innovation clients might not otherwise have access to as small, emerging businesses.

Mentoring:

A pool of volunteers selected based on their experience with the client’s industry sector and stage of development serve as mentors and business counselors for clients companies.

Professional Network

Cheetah innovation developed a broad-based pool of high-quality professionals that have the technical and business skills needed to support client businesses.

Advisory Boards:

Cheetah innovation developed a pool of professionals, technologists, and business owners that are willing to volunteer as advisory board members for client businesses. Such Cheetah innovation advisory boards are usually composed of three or four people who have experience and expertise in the technology/industry of the Cheetah innovation business. The advisory boards agree to meet with Cheetah innovation clients on a regular schedule and provide counsel at various stages of the client businesses’ development.

Capital and Financing Network:

Cheetah innovation established and maintained relationships with a network of banks, angel investors, venture capitalists, and corporate equity investors through capital networks, brokers, and personal contacts. Cheetah innovation provides introductions between incubated companies and appropriate investment resources.

Direct business development assistance

Business development assistance is the direct support provided to clients from the time of acceptance through graduation. The Cheetah innovation’s Executive Director would provide oversight and facilitate access to resources that meet client needs as those needs arise over the course of their incubation.

Hot Topic Seminars

Emphasizing topics of special interest to emerging technology businesses are provided on a monthly basis.

Business Topics Training:

Cheetah innovation works with professionals and local education institutions to provide incubator clients with access to the range of general business-related topics that are applicable to any start-up operation including financial, legal, organizational, marketing, insurance, etc.

Commercialization/Licensing Seminars:

Cheetah innovation hosts annual educational seminars that bring together technologists and entrepreneurs and discuss the challenges and rewards of technology commercialization, the processes and costs associated with licensing technologies, and area resources available to support those technology commercialization efforts.

Resource Library:

Cheetah innovation builds and maintains up-to-date information resources for technology start-ups including resource directories, business form templates, and checklists.

ICT capacity building

Beyond business company incubation, Cheetah innovation provides IT training courses for fresh graduates and public or private employees. This program mostly targets talented young programmers with no or less experience in industry, and we equip them with trending skills and best practice in software engineering.
