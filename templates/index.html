Who we are

ABOUT CHEETAH INNOVATION

CHEETAH INNOVATION is a network of technology project incubators that trains, supervises and supports project leaders and innovative ideas in Burundi, Democratic Republic of the Congo, Rwanda and Uganda.
CHEETAH INNOVATION is an initiative of the non-governmental organization IRIBA that aims to develop a culture of talent and entrepreneurship as an engine for Africa's development.
IRIBA breaks down the barriers to self-development that currently prevent young African entrepreneurs from realizing their ambitions.

Join us

MISSION

Our aim is to fight against unemployment among young Africans, contribute to the digital take-off of the continent through the promotion of technological innovation and help young entrepreneurs in the financing and implementation of their projects.

What we do

BUSINESS INCUBATOR & INCUBATOR IT

Cheetah Innovation is a support structure for young computer scientists with projects and innovative company creators. It intervenes from the formalization of the idea and the concept, until the creation of the company. Coached by professionals and experts, the young computer scientists can set up and test their project.

Cheetah Innovation is an incubator of digital companies allowing selected young people to:

  • Test the viability of their business project in real life, for 1 to 2 years; without social, financial or legal risk;
  • Benefit from a provisional status that allows them to charge for services and/or the sale of their products under the legal supervision of Cheetah Innovation;
  • Also benefit from a personalized accompaniment;
  • To have financial and logistical support.
